| T-02-SC | Tampering | npm/pip installs (kaggle / ucimlrepo / requests) | accept | all approved in 01-RESEARCH Package Legitimacy Audit (kaggle official CLI; ucimlrepo official UCI client); SUS verdict was the known unknown-downloads false positive — no checkpoint |
